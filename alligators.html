Alligator Facts



Alligators can stay underwater for up to 24 hours, they can do this by slowing down their heartrate and controlling their temperature.


Alligators can actually live for a year without food, they only use food for growing, recovering from injuries or reproduction.


Alligators love to nap in the sun, as you will see at Alligator Bay, if the sun is shining they're all out enjoying it!


Alligators are opportunistic hunters, they wait for their food and don't purposely go hunting.


Alligators rarely attack humans, this is because they don't see us as food. But that doesn't mean we're safe, you must always be careful around alligators.
