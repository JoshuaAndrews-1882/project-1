Information


Alligator Bay is located within the Everglades National Park in South Florida, a location called home by over 200,000 alligators!


Come to Alligator Bay and see our alligators, our lake is home to over 100 adult alligators and we also have an area to see our young alligators through different ages of their development.

Watch our experience performers enter the enclosure and brave the alligators, will they survive?

Get up close and personal with the alligators with an experience day you won't forget! Contact us for more information.
