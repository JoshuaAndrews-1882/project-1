Come Visit Us At Alligator Bay


Alligator Bay is located near to the Everglades National Park in South Florida, a location called home by over 200,000 alligators!


Come to Alligator Bay and see our alligators, our lake is home to over 100 adult alligators and we also have an area to see our young alligators through different ages of their development.


We have plenty for you to experience here, there is something for everyone.
Throughout the day we have live performances with the alligators where you can see our members of staff get close to these great creatures and show you how incredible they really are.
We have live feeding sessions, in which if you are lucky you will get chosen to participate in.


On site we have a range of food and drink at our on site cafe and a gift shop which if full of great items.
